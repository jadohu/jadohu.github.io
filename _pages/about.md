Hello, I am a Ph.D student at [Machine Learning and Intelligence Lab](https://mli.kaist.ac.kr/) (MLILAB) in KAIST, advised by [Prof. Eunho Yang](https://sites.google.com/site/yangeh/publications).

My recent research focuses on advancing the **reasoning capabilities** of large language models through reinforcement learning-based post-training methods. In parallel, I explore ways to improve the efficiency of foundation models, especially auto-regressive generative models, by optimizing inference-time performance via better memory usage, reduced latency, and techniques such as speculative decoding and knowledge distillation.
